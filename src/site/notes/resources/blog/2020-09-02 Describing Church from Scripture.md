Read Acts 14:21-28. Discuss Pauline cycle.

Read Acts 2:37-47. Discuss what the aspects of a church are.

The following passages each say something that describes church:

## Covenant / Family / Commitment
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Hebrews 10#10:19-25\|Hebrews 10#10:19-25]] :: Draw near with faith, hold fast our confession, encourage one another to love and do good, meet regularly together.
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Corinthians 1#1:10-17\|1 Corinthians 1#1:10-17]] :: Community, unity in Christ, harmony among households
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Acts 4#4:32-35\|Acts 4#4:32-35]] :: everything in common, distribution among the needy

## Baptism
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Romans 6#6:1-11\|Romans 6#6:1-11]] :: [[resources/concepts/Baptism\|Baptism]]
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Acts 8#8:35-38\|Acts 8#8:35-38]] :: [[resources/concepts/Baptism\|Baptism]], in the context of a god-fearer accepting the Messiah
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Peter 3#3:21\|1 Peter 3#3:21]] :: [[resources/concepts/Baptism\|baptism]] corresponds to flood narrative

## Word of God
- [[resources/bible/epistles/2 Peter 1#1:16-21\|2 Peter 1#1:16-21]] and [[resources/bible/epistles/2 Timothy 3#3:16-18\|2 Timothy 3#3:16-18]] :: The Bible is the foundation of our faith and practice. Private experience is secondary and subject to the Bible.
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Timothy 4#4:13\|1 Timothy 4#4:13]] :: Services to include reading the Bible, exhorting, teaching.
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Colossians 3#3:16\|Colossians 3#3:16]] :: teaching, admonishing, singing (psalms, hymns, spiritual songs), thankfulness

## Lord's Supper/Communion
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Matthew 26#26:26-29\|Matthew 26#26:26-29]], [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Corinthians 11#11:23-34\|1 Corinthians 11#11:23-34]] :: Pattern and principles for [[resources/concepts/Communion\|Communion]]

## Fellowship / Family
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Acts 2#2:38-47\|Acts 2#2:38-47]] :: Evangelize, Repent, believe, be [[resources/concepts/Baptism\|baptized]], receive the Holy Spirit, study Bible, spend time together, eat together ([[resources/concepts/Communion\|Communion]]), prayer. Hold all things in common, give to the needy, daily meet in public and in homes with joy, praising God.
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Colossians 3\|Colossians 3]] and [[resources/bible/epistles/Galatians 3#3:23\|Galatians 3#3:23]] :: new life in Christ and body composition, faith instead of law

## Giving & Ministry
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Corinthians 16#16:1-4\|1 Corinthians 16#16:1-4]] :: Giving to other churches, save money each week so that you are ready to give.

## Prayer
- [[resources/bible/epistles/James 5#5:13-16\|James 5#5:13-16]] :: Suffering, prayer, praise, sickness, confessing sin
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 John 1#1:9\|1 John 1#1:9]] :: 

## Praise
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Ephesians 5#5:18-20\|Ephesians 5#5:18-20]] :: be filled with the spirit, speak to one another with psalms, hymns, spiritual songs, giving thanks
- [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Corinthians 14#14:26-40\|1 Corinthians 14#14:26-40]], [[resources/bible/epistles/Colossians 3#3:16\|Colossians 3#3:16]] :: Orderly and participatory worship service, including: hymns, lessons, revelations, tongues and their interpretation all for mutual edification. Prophecies are to be weighed by others. Women at church and home

## Evangelism & Discipleship
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Acts 13#13:1-3\|Acts 13#13:1-3]] and [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Peter 2#2:9-10\|1 Peter 2#2:9-10]] :: Missions
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Matthew 28#28:18-20\|Matthew 28#28:18-20]] :: Make disciples of all peoples, [[resources/concepts/Baptism\|baptize]], teach the Bible, by power of Holy Spirit

## Leaders
- [[resources/bible/gospels/Acts 14#14:21-23\|Acts 14#14:21-23]] & [[resources/bible/epistles/1 Timothy 3#3:1-13\|1 Timothy 3#3:1-13]] :: Standards for elders and deacons.
- [[resources/bible/epistles/Ephesians 4#4:11-16\|Ephesians 4#4:11-16]] :: saints do ministry
