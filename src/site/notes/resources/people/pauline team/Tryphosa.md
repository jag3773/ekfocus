## Summary

Tryphosa is mentioned once in [[resources/bible/epistles/Romans 16#16:12\|Romans 16#16:12]], paired with [[resources/people/pauline team/Tryphena\|Tryphena]]: "Greet Tryphena and Tryphosa, who work hard in the Lord." Their similar names suggest a close relationship, likely sisters or freedwomen from the same household. See [[resources/people/pauline team/Tryphena\|Tryphena]] for the full discussion of their paired ministry and social background.
